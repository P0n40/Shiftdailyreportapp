# Quick Start Guide

## üöÄ Get Started in 3 Minutes

### Step 1: Sign Up (30 seconds)
1. Open the application
2. Click **"Don't have an account? Sign up"**
3. Enter:
   - Your name (e.g., "John Smith")
   - Email (e.g., "john@warehouse.com")
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Click **"Sign Up"**

You'll be automatically signed in!

### Step 2: Create Example Report (10 seconds)
1. Click the **"Add Example Report"** button
2. Wait for the success message
3. See your first report appear!

### Step 3: Explore (2 minutes)
1. **View the Report**
   - Click on the report card
   - See the clean, formatted preview
   - Click **"Save as PDF"** to download

2. **Edit the Report**
   - Click **"Edit"** button
   - Modify any section
   - Click **"Generate Report"** to save changes

3. **Create Your Own**
   - Click **"New Report"**
   - Fill in the form sections
   - Click **"Generate Report"**

---

## üìã Form Sections Explained

### Required Fields
- **Date**: When the shift occurred (defaults to today)
- **Location**: Your warehouse name or ID
- **Prepared By**: Your name (auto-filled from account)
- **Shift**: Day or Night

### Optional Sections (Add as needed)

**Staff on Shift**
- List all employees working this shift
- Add notes (role, special tasks, etc.)

**Daily Work**
- Log completed tasks
- Choose category (Picking, Packing, Loading, etc.)
- Describe what was done

**Critical Incidents**
- Document any safety or operational incidents
- Record actions taken to resolve

**Anomalies**
- Flag issues for the next shift
- Set severity: Info, Warning, or Critical
- Provide clear action items

**Support Required**
- Request help from maintenance, IT, etc.
- Auto-generates ticket numbers

## üí° Pro Tips

### Save Time
- Use **"Add Example Report"** to see a filled-out template
- Edit the example instead of starting from scratch
- Empty fields are automatically removed when saving

### Best Practices
- Fill out reports at end of shift while details are fresh
- Be specific in incident descriptions
- Include ticket numbers for all support requests
- Set appropriate severity levels for anomalies

### PDF Export
- Review the preview before exporting
- PDFs are named automatically: `Daily_Report_YYYY-MM-DD_Location_Shift.pdf`
- Perfect for printing or emailing to managers

## üîç Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to fill every section?**
A: No! Only Date, Location, Prepared By, and Shift are required. Add other sections as needed.

**Q: Can I edit a report after saving?**
A: Yes! Click "Edit" on any report to make changes.

**Q: How do I delete a report?**
A: Click the trash icon on the report card.

**Q: Can I duplicate a report?**
A: Not yet, but you can edit an existing report and change the date.

**Q: Is my data saved automatically?**
A: No, click "Generate Report" to save. The app will remember your session.
